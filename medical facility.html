Medical Facility

 

The College maintains an adequately equipped Medical Room. Every Student is required to undergo a routine medical check-up once a year. A well-qualified physician and a nurse attend to the needs of the students in the college. The Medical Report of the students examined by her is sent to the parents/guardians for their attention. Yearly report of the medical check-up is duly entered in their Identity Cards. FIRST AID FACILITY is also available for the students.

Apart from these basic amenities, several talk sessions are organized time and again, wherein Doctors and Experts give their insights on certain health related issues and general well-being

Convenor: Ms. RENU GUPTA
