Library and Reading Room

Librarian : Ms. Bhawna Singh

Library Facilities

The Library plays a significant role as a teaching-learning resource centre for the faculty and students. Our library is fully computerized with the catalogue available in OPAC (On Line Public Access Catalogue) form for easy and quick access to all resources. Users can check the availability of books and journals in the library from various terminals, and then procure them for issue and reference.

The library has a rich collection of text books and reference material, which includes encyclopedias, yearbooks, dictionaries, atlases, general knowledge books, biographies, autobiographies and latest fiction in English and Hindi languages. Our collections include nearly 82888 books, periodicals subscriptions (academic journals and magazines, 10 daily newspapers in English and Hindi, and electronic resources including online databases through UGC INFONET, N-LIST, CDs, DVDs, e-journals, e-books). A book bank that runs with the help of grants from IPS helps the students from economically weaker sections. It has a unique Braille books collection. Recorders and laptops with special software are issued to differently-abled students for the entire duration of their courses.

Information through e-resources can be accessed in the Information Centre. Free access to more than 4000 journals with abstracts and full text information, e-resources, e-books, e-databases are available through UGC INFONET. The Centre in the library has 25 computers with internet facility to help students with their research work. Library is member of “ National Library and Information Services Infrastructure for Scholarly Content” (N-LIST). N-LIST project provides access to e-resources to faculty, researchers and students from colleges. The authorized users from college can apply for this and get password to access e-resources and download articles required by them directly from the Publisher’s website once they are duly authenticated as authorized users through INFLIBNET Centre. Library premises are a Wi-Fi enabled zone for all users. Library organizes during every academic session a two days book exhibition by publishers and distributors in the college premises. Besides giving an opportunity to students to purchase books, it also facilitates purchase of books for the library by the various departments. Library organizes during every academic session an orientation programme/ online talk/webinar on information literacy about e-resources or any other subject related to awareness about use of library resources/e-resources etc.

Some more information about the library:

  • Total area of the library is 7116.32 sq.m.
  • The library has a seating capacity of 150 students and also has reading room for the faculty.
  • The library opens at 8.30 am and closes at 5.30 pm on all the days except Saturday and Sunday.
  • The Library has three floors.
Ground Floor: The ground floor of the building consists of Librarian’s room, Technical Section, Circulation Desk, OPAC area, Reference & Text Book stack area, Braille Collection, International Punjabi Society Book Bank section for economically weaker students, and the Question Papers and Syllabi section.

Second Floor: Circulation Desk for 2nd Floor Books, Reserve Books section, Reading Hall, Gratis books section for donated books, Xerox. Reading Material Section, Question Papers and Syllabi Section for Students, Separate Reading Room for B.El.Ed and B.Ed. Students, Stack Area.
