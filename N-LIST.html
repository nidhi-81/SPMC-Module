ABOUT N-LIST

N-LIST: The project entitled “National Library and Information Services Infrastructure for Scholarly Content”, this was jointly executed by the e-ShodhSindhu Consortium, INFLIBNET Centre and the INDEST-AICTE Consortium, IIT Delhi. This is a special databases provided by INFLIBNET meant only for college libraries. The N-LIST project provides access to e-resources to faculty, researchers and students from colleges. The authorized users from college can access e-resources and download articles required by them directly from the Publisher’s website once they are duly authenticated as authorized users through INFLIBNET Centre
