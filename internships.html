                                                     Vskills Internship Programme

Vskills, a Govt. of India and Govt. of NCT Delhi Co-initiative is inviting students for its virtual internship program in various acknowledged  fields such as Finance, HR, Media, Economics, Journalism, Banking, Web Development, Quality Management and many more.

Our company is widely acknowledged for its internship portal where we give the students an opportunity to work while getting acquainted with the practical implications of their subjects.

The students work and research on various subjects where apart from applying their knowledge and skills, we give them the chance of gaining professional experience in their fields so that they come out with a different personality full of confidence and all grilled for the corporate world.

Vskills runs its flagship internship programme where bright interns work academic council at Vskills to create question banks on various topics. Vskills quiz portal boasts of thousands of questions and is the most extensive quizzing portal having thousands of questions on various topic on employable skills.

What you need ?

  1. Enjoy a good quiz and would love to create questions on various topics
  2. Have a burning desire to add value on a mission which is the largest of its kind.
  3. Have analytical skills and have an eye for detail
  4. Great attitude to learn
  5. Great communication skills

What you do ?

  1. Interns chose a  topic of their choice from the list provided  for eg. Human resources
  2. They are expected to contribute 200 questions with multiple choice answers and explanations for each question

What you get ?

  1. Certificate of appreciation if you complete the desired number of questions
  2. Certificate of Merit if your questions get selected for the final portal
  3. Work closely with the academic council to gain exposure on assessments and business communication skills
  4. Exclusive discount on a Vskills Certification

For more details, contact  Ambika at ambika.sharma@vskills.in.
